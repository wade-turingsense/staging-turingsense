Our History

Our founding team, elite-level athletes in tennis, taekwondo, and swimming, knew there had to be a better way to learn the complicated movement that's required in most athletic and even recreational activities.

Motion capture technology offered trainees a potential breakthrough: the chance to see their movement on all sides of their body, in real time. But in its optical form, motion capture is affordable only to movie studios making blockbuster movies. Our founders' insight, as former hardware engineers, was that the expensive optical approach could be replaced with digital technology. And so PIVOT was born.
